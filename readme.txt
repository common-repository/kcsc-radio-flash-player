A widget that displays a flash player for KCSCRadio.com that streams the KCSC radio feed directly to the user's browser.

== Description ==

KCSC is California State University, Chico's internet radio station. Student DJ's broadcast a variety of programs including alternative music, sports talk and much more. Student owned and operated since 1951.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `kcsc-radio-flash-player` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Drag the KCSC Flash Player widget to your sidebar through the 'Appearance' settings in Wordpress

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Why would I want this widget? =

So that your users can listen to KCSC while browsing your site, which could significantly increase the amount of time spent on your site.
